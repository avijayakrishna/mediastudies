Shwetha M. P (HOD Dept of M.Sc Electronic Media )

Here is a lecturer who reaches students very easily with her pleasant voice and kindness. She efficiently trains students in visual communication, film communication, radio program production and anchoring techniques. She is Mrs Shwetha M. P.

She did her M.Sc in Electronic Media at Sri Siddhartha Centre for Media Studies, Tumkur University, with first rank in 2007. She also Qualified K-SET in 2014.

She has worked as a production assistant in Doordarshan Kendra, Bangalore. She has also worked with the news section as script writer.

She has done “Route voice guide” project For Tumkur City Bus Service. She has given many Lectures as a Resource person in different universities.

Her voice is her strength. A program for children was telecasted in Doordarshana-Chandana, with her anchoring. She has conceptualized many series of programs for Radio Siddhartha (campus radio) and she has been announcing special programs almost Three years. She has participated in live phone-in program for Akhila Bharatha Kannada Sahitya Sammelana, held in Chitradurga. She has narrated many short stories, talks for the radio and directed a radio drama, involving students. She gives voice to radio jingles, commercials and announcements. She has received Vani Certificate, by All India Radio, Bangalore. Radio is her forte.

Monolith Rock of Madhugiri is a documentary she has made, which grabbed the attention of state government. It is about a single rock wonder in Madhugiri, Tumkur district in Karnataka. She also writes number of articles and done Radio programmes for Radio Siddhartha. Presented Seminar papers at Different National and international Seminars.
